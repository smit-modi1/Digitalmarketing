About Us

How Point Marketing works as a growth platform

Point Marketing is a UK-focused affiliate and digital commerce platform built around two outcomes: helping shoppers discover worthwhile products faster, and helping brands generate traction through cleaner offer positioning.

For buyers, the goal is simple navigation, strong product summaries, and direct routes to purchase. For business clients, the model is built around discounted tester pushes, influencer seeding, offer-led landing pages, and better visibility for Amazon and TikTok-friendly products.

We think like operators, not just publishers: every featured item should be easy to understand, easy to click, and aligned with a category that people are already ready to buy in.

Privacy & Policy

Clear policies for affiliate transparency

This site uses affiliate links to Amazon.co.uk. Clicking a product may earn a referral commission if you complete a qualifying purchase. That does not change the price you pay.

Contact form submissions are stored only to help respond to enquiries from buyers or business clients. No checkout or payment data is collected by this site, and purchases are completed on third-party marketplaces.

Administrative product controls are restricted to the site operator. Public visitors cannot add, edit, or delete product listings through the storefront.
